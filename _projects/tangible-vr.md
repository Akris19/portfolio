Imagine building with physical LEGO bricks and feeling that build continues inside VR. This project explores how tangible inputs can make virtual environments feel more direct, playful, and spatially grounded.

## Idea

The goal was to connect real-world construction with a digital scene so the user could assemble, inspect, and interact with a model across both physical and virtual space.
